Who We Are
Creating Change in Rabuor, Migori County
100+ Local Jobs

A Chinese-Kenyan Aquaculture Partnership

Ken Tilapia is a large-scale tilapia aquaculture operation in Rabuor Village, Migori County, on the shores of Lake Victoria. We combine Chinese aquaculture technology with Kenyan community expertise.

With over 30 fish cages, 20 fish ponds, and 100+ employees, we are transforming livelihoods in Rabuor Village. We also supply quality fish feed to support local fish farmers.

All tilapia is harvested daily and sold at a flat KSh 310 per kilogram across all four size grades.
