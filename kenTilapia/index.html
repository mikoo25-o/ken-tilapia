Who We Are
Creating Change in Rabuor, Migori County

A Chinese-Kenyan Aquaculture Partnership

Ken Tilapia is a large-scale tilapia aquaculture operation located in Rabuor Village, Migori County, Kenya — on the shores of Lake Victoria. We combine Chinese aquaculture technology with Kenyan community expertise.

With over 30 fish cages, 20 fish ponds, and a growing team of 100+ employees, we are transforming livelihoods in Rabuor Village and contributing to Migori County's economy.

All our tilapia is responsibly farmed, harvested fresh daily, and available in four size grades at a flat price of KSh 310 per kilogram.
